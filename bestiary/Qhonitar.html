Qhonitar

Large Megabeast

A large creature resembling some fusion of an ant and a scorpion, but blown up to the size of a mountain lion. It crawls on six legs and has a long tail tipped with a stinger. It's mandibles are strong enough to chop off a limb. It tries to poison its prey by stinging it before biting.

The qhonitar's name means "forest ghost" in Izil and it is not native to this world.

Sting (AP Cost: 5) [Damage: 3d6 Piercing]

The qhonitar stings one target within 4m. On hit, the target must make a DC10 FOR check or for the shorter of 2 full combat turns or passing a DC12 FOR check.

Bite (AP Cost: 6) [Damage: 3d6 Rending]

The qhonitar bites one target in its forward arc.

Traits

Alien War Animal - The qhonitar is Skilled in Acrobatics, Athletics, and Sneak, gaining a +4 to those skill checks.

Natural Armor - The qhonitar gains 2 damage reduction to Rending, Piercing, and Crushing damage sources.
