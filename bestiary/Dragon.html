Dragon

Gigantic Legendary

A large, winged magical ancestor of lizards. Dragons are fiercely territorial, are known to speak to sapients in multiple languages, are big enough to swallow a cow whole and strong enough to destroy a sturdy log cabin simply by smashing into it. They are covered in tough scales that bold hunters covet and turn into protective armor, although very few hunters are successful.

Attacks

Can use techniques: False

Breathe Fire (AP Cost: 12) [Damage: 6d10 Fire]

The dragon breathes fire in a 12m long straight line. When a creature is caught in the fire it can make a DC10 Acrobatics check to take half of the damage. The creature also gains the Burning status effect for 5 turns.

Bite (AP Cost: 8) [Damage: 5d12 Rending]

The dragon bites one target adjacent to it. It can pay 2 more AP per round to hold it in its jaws until the dragon takes damage, dropping the creature. Additionally, it can pay 2 more AP to throw the creature, which travels 1d12 x 2m, taking 1d6 Crushing damage for every 2m traveled.

Tail Swipe (AP Cost: 12) [Damage: 5d10 Crushing]

The dragon swipes at an arc of 3 tiles within 4m of it using its tail. Creatures hit by the sweep fall prone and are knocked back 2m.

Body Slam (AP Cost: 16) [Damage: 10d10 Crushing]

The dragon can forcefully land if in the air, crushing all targets beneath it.

Traits

Dragonhide

Dragons take half damage from all sources.

Frightening Presence

Whenever a dragon flies or breathes fire, all creatures within 60m that can see it must make a SPR check or gain the Afraid status effect for 1d4 turns.

Poisonous Blood

A dragon's blood is a very potent toxin that can be absorbed through the skin. A couple of drops relaxes and numbs the area, making a good painkiller, but a small vial's worth, like the kind sold at an alchemist's shop, will kill any human. Dragon hunters fighting it in melee commonly wear full-face helmets to protect against splashing blood. Whenever a dragon is harmed in melee, make a DC5 (or DC1 if you are wearing full-body armor and some sort of face mask - doesn't need to be metal) AGI check or take 2d10 damage.
