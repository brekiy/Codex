Traits

Beast of Burden

A horse can carry 80kg as a light load. With another 80kg (160kg), it carries a medium load, and at 240kg it carries a heavy load. It can't carry more than a heavy load.

Warhorse

A warhorse gains +2 to MGT and FOR, and increaes its load limits to 90/180/270kg for light/medium/heavy loads.
