Bestiary

The bestiary contains information on all the creatures in the known world. It lists their SPAMFIC stats, methods of attack, and traits, as well as basic descriptions. Each creature has its own page.

Sapients that aren't playable are given stats befitting a human variant. GMs creating encounters are encouraged to change up the loadouts, perks and/or traits of them to fit the playable species if they wish.
