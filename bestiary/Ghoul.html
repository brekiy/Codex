Ghoul

Medium Megabeast

A malformed victim of a vampire attack that didn't die. They are consumed with a lust for flesh and will attack anything, even other ghouls, if they are hungry enough.

Traits

Poison Immunity - Ghouls are immune to all poisons and the Poisoned status effect.

Vulnerability - Ghouls take double damage from fire and cold iron.

Necrophage - The ghoul can take a bite of a corpse, instantly healing 1d6 Wounds. This cannot bring it below 0 Wounds (full health).
