Bite (AP Cost: 4) [Damage: 1d10 Rending]

The spider bites its target with its fangs, pumping it full of venom. The target must make a DC10 FOR save or instantly take 1 additional Wound and gain the Poisoned status effect.

Shoot Web (AP Cost: 8) [Damage: 0]

The spider shoots a sticky glob of silk at a target up to 10m away. If it hits, the target is restrained by webbing, and must pass a DC10 MGT check to break out of it or be freed by another, using a source of Rending or Fire damage.
