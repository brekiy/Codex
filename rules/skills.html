Skills System

Every character has a set of general skills, some of which they are proficient in and some of which they aren't. For a given skill, there are three levels of expertise:

  • Unskilled: The character gains no bonuses.
  • Skilled: The character gains a +4 bonus.
  • Expert: The character gains a +6 bonus and Advantage.

It costs the same for all skills to upgrade their expertise levels. To become Skilled it costs 4PP, and to become an Expert it costs 10PP.

A given skill has up to two modifiers. When performing a skill check, a character picks the higher of the modifiers.
